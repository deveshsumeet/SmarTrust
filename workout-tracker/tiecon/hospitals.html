Alameda Health System

Alameda Health System (AHS), formerly Alameda County Medical Center (ACMC), operates 4 hospitals (Highland Hospital, John George Psychiatric Hospital, Fairmont Hospital, San Leandro Hospital) and four primary care medical clinics (called ambulatory Wellness Centers) within the County.In March 2013, ACMC officially changed its name to Alameda Health System.
